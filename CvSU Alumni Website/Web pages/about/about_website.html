This website was developed for educational purposes as part of a school project. It is locally hosted on a server within the local network, without the use of an online domain.

What information do we collect?

In our data collection process, we exclusively gather specific information essential for our purposes. This includes the graduation pictures of each member, their personal data, and the provided photo templates.

The scope of collected data is deliberately limited to these elements, ensuring that our information gathering aligns precisely with the requirements of our project or initiative. Transparency and respect for privacy are paramount considerations in our data collection practices, and we strive to adhere to ethical standards and legal obligations in handling the gathered information.

Use of Information

The information collected, encompassing graduation pictures, personal data, and provided photo templates, serves specific and predefined purposes within our operational framework. It is utilized exclusively for the intended objectives of our project, ensuring the effective execution and fulfillment of its goals.

Our commitment to privacy and ethical data handling dictates that the gathered information is employed responsibly, safeguarding the confidentiality and rights of the individuals involved.

Additionally, we adhere to relevant legal regulations and ethical standards to guarantee the proper use and protection of the collected data, fostering a trustworthy and secure environment for our stakeholders.
